Our Story

Founded in 2020, Ngugi E-Car Sales emerged from a vision to make electric vehicles accessible to everyone. We believe that the future of transportation is electric, and we're committed to helping our customers make the transition with confidence. Our team of experts has decades of combined experience in the automotive industry, specializing in electric and hybrid vehicles. We understand the unique needs of EV buyers and provide comprehensive support throughout the entire journey. Today, we're proud to be one of the most trusted names in electric vehicle sales, with thousands of satisfied customers driving cleaner, more efficient vehicles.
